Co je Share of Search?

Share of Search je metrika vyvinuta Lesem Binetem (IPA, 2020), ktera meri podil znacky na celkovem brandovem vyhledavani v dane kategorii. Vyzkum ukazuje, ze Share of Search vysvetluje 83 % variability trzniho podilu (James Hankins, IPA).

"Share of Search has enormous potential and predictive power to track brands and advertising going forward."
-- Les Binet, IPA EffWorks 2020
"Share of search is a simple and elegant alternative to share of voice -- it measures real consumer demand, not just media spend."
-- Mark Ritson, Marketing Week
"When Share of Search exceeds market share, brand growth typically follows within 6-12 months."
-- James Hankins, IPA Think Tank

Metodika a zdroje

Share of Search (SoS) meri podil brandoveho vyhledavani kazde znacky na celkovem objemu brandovych dotazu v dane kategorii. Koncept vyvinul Les Binet v roce 2020 jako levnou a rychlou alternativu ke Share of Voice.
